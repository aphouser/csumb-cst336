Welcome to Mercury!


Mercury is the first planet in our solar system, closest to the sun. Even though it is closest to the sun, it isn't the hottest, that is Venus.

The spin of Mercury is quite slow, taking 59 Earch days to make one full rotation - that's a long work day! And, it gets even worse. Couple the orbit around the sun, and that's only 1 sunrise every 180 Earth days!!
