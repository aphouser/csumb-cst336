Welcome to Earth!


Earth is the third planet in our solar system, and the only place we know to support life. This is because it is the only planet we know of to have an atmosphere that contains free oxygen and oceans of water.

Earth is the largest of the rocky planets (the others being Mercury, Venus, and Mars), and smaller than all the gas giants.

Our position relative to the sun, how far out we orbit, is what makes it possible for life to exist here. We're in the sweet spot where water can exist in liquid form. Too close and it will evaporate, too far and it'll be ice.
