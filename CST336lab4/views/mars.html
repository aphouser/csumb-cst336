Welcome to Mars!

Mars is the fourth planet in our solar system. Named after the Roman god of war, it is often described as the “Red Planet”, as the iron oxide prevalent on its surface gives it a reddish appearance.

Mars is a planet with surface features similar to the impact craters of the Moon and the volcanoes, valleys, deserts, and polar ice caps of Earth. It is also the site of Olympus Mons, the tallest known mountain on a planet within our Solar System.
